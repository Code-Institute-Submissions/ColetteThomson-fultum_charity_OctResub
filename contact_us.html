A bit about us

Fultum Wiltshire was been inspiring men, women and organisations to donate their time and funding to assist the people of Wiltshire, for over 100 years. The determined and devoted force behind the organisation has been our founder Lady Elspeth Fitzgerald. A very charismatic, big-hearted person and most generous soul, our 'Lady E' left her country estate to the Fultum Wiltshire community support organisation.

Our name 'Fultum' is taken directly from the Latin word (of middle English origin), and its meaning is 'to help; support; and protect'.

Work for us - we need Volunteers!

If you have some spare time, we urgently need:

  • Drivers (for our Fultum Shuttle Service or local individual lifts to supermarkets, doctors etc)
  • Companions (for home visits and onsite companionship)
  • Part-time staff (to work in our offices or our Fultum Cafe)
  • Hobby/crafts/cookery teachers (for our many workshops)
  • Business teachers (for our various life skills workshops)
  • Book readers (for our sight-impaired members, or members that just enjoy being read to)

Or any other way you think you may be able to assist.
